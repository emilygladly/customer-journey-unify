3. Sequence Content by Readiness Level

Each RL has different goals, content themes, and cadence. Within each, we personalize by industry + persona.

RL3: Awareness Sequence

Goal: Get them to engage
Cadence: 5 touches over 6 weeks

Touch 1: Industry benchmark / trend
Touch 2: Thought leadership article
Touch 3: "Did you see this?" (relevant news)
Touch 4: Peer company mention
Touch 5: Soft CTA (webinar, guide)

RL2: Engagement Sequence

Goal: Drive deeper engagement / MQL
Cadence: 6 touches over 4 weeks

Touch 1: Follow up on their activity
Touch 2: Relevant case study
Touch 3: Guide/playbook offer
Touch 4: Webinar invite
Touch 5: ROI angle
Touch 6: Soft meeting ask

RL1: Meeting Sequence

Goal: Book meeting
Cadence: 5 touches over 2 weeks

Touch 1: Direct meeting ask + value prop
Touch 2: Quick case study + meeting ask
Touch 3: ROI calculator offer
Touch 4: Peer reference offer
Touch 5: "Worth 15 min?" final ask

The Complete Picture

1. ENTRY: Account enters via TAL addition, event list, or high-value page visit

2. CLASSIFY: Check Readiness Level (RL1, RL2, RL3)

3. SEQUENCE: Unify runs appropriate sequence, personalized by industry + persona

4. MONITOR: Watch for engagement, MQL, or response

5. HANDOFF: MQL or RL1 response â†’ Rep takes over

6. OUTCOME: Meeting â†’ Opp, or No traction â†’ Marketing Nurture â†’ Re-enter later

The key principle: Automation handles everything until MQL or RL1 response. Rep only works qualified/high-intent accounts. Everyone else stays in the machine.
