Unified Customer Journey Map

Mapping Account Status + Readiness Level + Contact Status → Tool & Content Selection

The Three Frameworks

Account Status

"Who should we target?"

Account-level. Where is this account in their journey?

  • Inactive
  • Engaged
  • Active Outreach
  • Open Opportunity
  • Customer

Readiness Level

"How much should we invest?"

Marketing segmentation. Budget & channel allocation.

  • RL1 (Sales Ready) - HIGH
  • RL2 (Aware) - MEDIUM
  • RL3 (Unaware) - LOW

Contact Status

"What message should we send?"

Person-level. How engaged is this contact?

  • New → Prospect → MQL
  • Working → SQL
  • Customer
  • Nurture / Deprioritized
How they connect:
Account Status tells us where they are → Readiness Level tells us how much to invest → Contact Status tells us what to say

Contact Status = Message Personalization Layer

Within any Account Status, individual contacts have their own status that determines messaging:

Contact Status Definition Message Strategy
New Just created, no engagement yet Intro messaging, value prop, brand awareness
Prospect Campaign response, meets basic fit Follow up on specific interest, offer more content
MQL "Cream of the crop" - high fit + high engagement Direct outreach, demo offers, case studies for their vertical
Working Rep actively communicating 1:1 personalized, reference their specific pain points
SQL Added to opportunity Deal-specific content, technical deep dives, ROI justification
Nurture Not ready; needs more nurturing Drip content, stay top of mind, don't push for meeting

The Decision Logic

WHEN new account enters TAL:
→ Account Status = Inactive
→ Readiness = RL3
→ Tool = Unify (cold outreach, thought leadership)
WHEN account shows engagement (website, email, ad click):
→ Account Status changes to Engaged
→ Readiness = RL2 (or RL1 if high-intent signal)
→ Tool = Outreach (high-tier) or Unify (lower-tier)
WHEN contact becomes MQL or Sales contacts them:
→ Account Status changes to Active Outreach
→ Readiness = RL1
→ Tool = Outreach.io (rep-led, 1:1)
WHEN opportunity is created:
→ Account Status = Open Opportunity
→ Tool = Outreach.io (deal management, multi-threading)
WHEN deal is closed-lost or account goes cold:
→ Account Status = Nurture (60 day cooling)
→ Readiness resets to RL3
→ Tool = Marketo (drip nurture)

The Key Insight

Account Status Drives Tool Selection

  • Inactive → Unify (automated cold)
  • Engaged → Unify or Outreach (by tier)
  • Active Outreach → Outreach.io (rep-led)
  • Open Opp → Outreach.io (deal mgmt)
  • Nurture → Marketo (drip)

Contact Status Drives Content Selection

  • New → Brand awareness, value prop
  • Prospect → Follow-up on interest
  • MQL → Demo offers, case studies
  • Working/SQL → 1:1 personalized
  • Nurture → Drip, don't push

Readiness Level determines HOW MUCH to invest at each stage.
